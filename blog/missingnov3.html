Anyway, a bit of behind the scenes, so when i first started making it i already had a concept in mind.


The Original Concept


The original concept was for the camera to scroll down from a view of the night-time-ish moon to a animation of BF throwing a Pokeball into the water before singing.


However i decided that i wanted a different intro, so i listened to the audio for when BF starts singing and i wrote a simple script that you will see in the dev video, that played the animation of the camera panning down to focus on BF.


I also decided that a side-on view wouldn't work for the idea i had in mind, so i changed it so BF's back is facing the camera throughout the song.


Feedback


After uploading i got mainly one consistent bit of feedback, and that was to switch the way notes are represented. Instead of having them as a 3D object, i will be switching the notes to a canvas so that they are always facing the player's screen.
